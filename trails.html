Tiger Mountain

image credit: https://www.topchinatravel.com

Tiger Mountain is located outside of Issaquah and is just shy of 2 miles out yet possibly the most scenic trail. The hiking trail is has a slight elevation of 1100 feet making the hike relatively easy.

Mount Rainier Summerland Trail

image credit: https://www.jigzone.com

Mount Rainier Summerland Trails provide hikers and runners with multiple choices that vary in elevation and distance. Although most trails are used for hiking some people use them for a jog. The longest of the Summerland Trails exend out to 15 miles with a peak of 6800ft.

Burke Gilman

image credit: https://imagesus-ssl.homeaway.com

The Burke Gilman Trail is a flat in/out trail in Seattle. This trail provides the people of Seattle with a versatile course to use for many activities such as biking, hiking, rollerblading and running. The Burke Gilman Trail is open to the public.

I-5 Colonnade

image credit:https://www.notey.com

I-5 Colonade is the first ever urban mountain bike park in Seattle and is a part of the City of Seattle Parks. The park consists of novice, intermediate and advanced courses.
